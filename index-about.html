Drilling ROP Optimizer



The background for this web application is based on academic research by John Abish, conducted under the guidance of Dr. Robello Samuel. The research has been selected for publication by SPE as a part of their WRM 2020 Conference journal.

This web application was developed as a means to learn and showcase the potential of cloud systems and their role in sustainable energy production.
