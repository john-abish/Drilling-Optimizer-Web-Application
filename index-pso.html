Particle Swarm Optimization

Particle swarm optimization (PSO) is a metaheuristic technique for optimization based on the stochastic optimization method. It is a type of swarm intelligence, modelled on the behavior exhibited by flocks of birds or schools of fish. Each ‘bird’ in the swarm is a particle which moves through the solution space within given constraints to find an optimal solution. The particles represent randomly generated solutions to the function. The solution particless navigate guided by rules governing their velocity and position. PSO is initiated with a set of random particles which search for the global best solution until a predetermined number of iterations is reached.
