Volve Field Data

The Volve oil field is located 200 kilometres west of Stavanger at the southern end of the Norwegian sector. It was decommissioned in September 2016 after 8.5 years in operation. The real-time drilling dataset used in this project was obtained from the Volve field through Equinor open-source datasets. An appraisal well, 15/9-F-15, was chosen for analysis, with data obtained from a true vertical depth of 5,340 ft to 5,940 ft.
